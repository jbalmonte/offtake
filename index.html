Documentation

Below shows the important progress happened during the discussion and their respective task assignment

To divide the work amongst us, we posted tasks to our group messenger chat, and you may put your name anywhere you like on that assignment. We conceived of it in this manner so that everyone of us would enjoy it, and it was compatible with the responsibilities allocated to each of us.

Next, we agreed to choose the business that sells PC units/parts because we believe it's the most fittest in our objective and skills.

After that, the first thing our group did was come up with a name for our company: cyberix, wired, offtake, cybersaur, cyberque, and cyberlab are a few examples. The first one we agreed on was cyberix, but we discovered on the internet that both cyberix and wired were already in use as business names. So we simply went ahead and selected the remainder of our alternatives. The second and final item we settled on for our company name was "offtake."

We chose to design the logo for our company after deciding on a name for it. We had two logo possibilities, and we all decided on the second.

Now that we've settled on the logo we'll use, designed by Marco and Patulot, each of us has begun to perform our bit. And then we compiled it onto the Google Drive that we created. After compiling, we then proceeded to make a WIX website and an additional PPT to present our outputs, step by step procedures and individual reflections. Finally, after doing all that, we submitted all the files needed.
